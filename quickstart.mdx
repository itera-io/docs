## Setup your environment

Learn how to get started with Taikun CloudWorks platform.

### Prerequisites

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion icon="cloud" title="Cloud Provider Account">
    You'll need an account with one of our supported cloud providers:
    - [Google Cloud Platform](/Documentation/Topics/Supported%20Cloud%20Providers/Google%20Cloud%20Platform)
    - [Amazon Web Services](/Documentation/Topics/Supported%20Cloud%20Providers/Amazon%20Web%20Services)
    - [Microsoft Azure](/Documentation/Topics/Supported%20Cloud%20Providers/Microsoft%20Azure)
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion icon="user" title="Taikun Account">
    1. Visit [Taikun Dashboard](https://app.taikun.cloud)
    2. Create a new account or sign in with your existing credentials
    3. Follow our [User Management Guide](/Documentation/Topics/Account%20Management/User%20Types) for role setup
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### First Steps

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion icon="rocket" title="Create Your First Project">
  Follow our step-by-step guide to [create your first project](/Documentation/Topics/Managing%20your%20Projects/Create%20a%20Project).
  This will help you:
  1. Set up your cloud credentials
  2. Configure your project settings
  3. Deploy your first cluster
</Accordion>
<Accordion icon="cube" title="Deploy Kubernetes">
  Learn how to [create and manage Kubernetes clusters](/Documentation/Topics/Getting%20Started/Creating%20Kubernetes%20cluster)
  with Taikun's intuitive interface.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
