If you registered your account on Taikun webpage (Taikun.cloud), you can quickly check your subscription status by following these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Log in to your account on our webpage.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Second Step">
    Click on the **Subscription tab** in the left-hand menu
  </Step>

  <Step title="Third Step">
    You will see your current subscription plan and the expiration date of your subscription.
  </Step>
</Steps>

The Subscription menu lets you switch between various subscriptions by clicking on the chosen option.

<Note>If you purchased your plan through one of our partners, you will need to reach out to that partner to make any changes to your account</Note>

## Monitor used TCUs

To calculate your resource usage, you can access the Usage Reports section. Up there, you can see the total amount of TCUs spent in your account and calculate the pricing of the resources you use.
