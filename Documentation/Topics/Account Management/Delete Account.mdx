# Overview

You can delete an account in Taikun by following these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Head to your Profile section in the top right corner
  </Step>

  <Step title="Second Step">
    Press the “Delete account” icon
  </Step>

  <Step title="Third Step">
    Click “yes, delete“.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  ![](https://mintlify.s3-us-west-1.amazonaws.com/taikuncloud/Documentation/Images/Account%20Management/delete.webp)
</Frame>

<Warning>**The action can’t be undone. Make sure you want to delete your account.**</Warning>
