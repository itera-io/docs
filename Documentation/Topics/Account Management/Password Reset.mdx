To reset your Taikun password follow these steps:

To start, you’ll need to go to the page where you can reset your password – Press the “Forgot your password?” link on the login page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Navigate to the password reset page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Second Step">
    Enter your email address or username
  </Step>

  <Step title="Third Step">
    Check your email for reset instructions
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Frame caption="Forgot your password?">
  ![](https://mintlify.s3-us-west-1.amazonaws.com/taikuncloud/Documentation/Images/Account%20Management/password.1.webp)
</Frame>

Message from Taikun will include a link to a password reset page that you can use to reset your password.
