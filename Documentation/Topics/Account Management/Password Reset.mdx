To reset your Taikun password follow these steps:

To start, you’ll need to go to the page where you can reset your password – [https://app.taikun.cloud/forgotten-password.](https://app.taikun.cloud/forgotten-password.) Alternatively, press the “Forgot your password?” link on the login page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Navigate to the password reset page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Second Step">
    Enter your email address or username
  </Step>

  <Step title="Third Step">
    Check your email for reset instructions
  </Step>
</Steps>
