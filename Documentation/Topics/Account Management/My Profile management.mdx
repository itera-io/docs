# Access My Profile

To access the My Profile menu, click on your Profile in the top-right corner of Taikun. Up there you will be able to:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="View information">
    View information about your profile such as User name, Organization, e-mail, and a Role
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Change settings">
   Change your profile password and email address
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Enable or disable">
    Enable or disable email notifications and Demo mode
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
