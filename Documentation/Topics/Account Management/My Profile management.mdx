# Access My Profile

To access the My Profile menu, click on your Profile in the top-right corner of Taikun. Up there you will be able to:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="View Information">
    View information about your profile such as User name, Organization, e-mail, and a Role
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Change settings">
    Change your profile password and email address
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Enable or Disable">
    Enable or disable email notifications and Demo mode
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

# Partners view

Partners will be able to see information about their Partner Organization. Custom Logos and Backgrounds can be applied there as well.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Profile Information">
    To view your profile information, including your user name, organization, email, and role, press on your user name in the top right corner.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Change Settings">
    To change your profile password and email address, go to the “Security” section of your account settings. Simply enter your current password and your new password or email address to make the updates.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Enable or Disable">
    To enable or disable email notifications and demo mode, go to the “Notifications” section of your account settings. Simply toggle the switches to the desired settings.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Partner Access">
    If you have a Partner access, you can view your partner organization information by going to the “Partner Organization” section of your account settings. From here, you can view details about your Organization and customize your Organization’s logo and background.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
